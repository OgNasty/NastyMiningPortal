NASTYFANS MINTED SEATS

THE FUNDRAISER THAT KEEPS GIVING

Created and distributed by NastyMining as a fundraiser for NastyFans, Minted Seats are unique coins made with silver and gold. They act as physical Bitcoin wallets while at the same time being tied to a virtual NastyFans seat. This entitles the coin to a share of the NastyFans weekly Bitcoin distribution. Since it's private key is unknown by any individual, coins with their security hologram in tact can be traded with consideration to bitcoins held at the coin's address.

ENGRAVED KEYS

Sequential vanity addresses unique to each coin style are generated using an offline computer and engraved onto each coin before being secured with a custom tamper evident hologram.
